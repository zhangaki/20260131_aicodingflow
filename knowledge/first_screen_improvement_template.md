## First Screen Improvement Template

**Goal:** Reduce bounce rate by improving the first screen experience.

**Steps:**

1.  **Identify the Target Page:** Specify the URL or content of the page to be improved.
2.  **Analyze the Current Intro and Headline:**
    *   **Problem:** Is the problem clearly and specifically stated?
    *   **Outcome:** Is the desired outcome clearly stated and quantified?
    *   **Next Step:** Is there a clear and compelling next step for the user?
    *   **Style:** Is the style concrete and free of generic claims and AI clich√©s?
    *   **Headline Alignment:** Does the intro support the headline's promise?
3.  **Rewrite the Intro:**
    *   Focus on clearly stating the problem, desired outcome, and next step.
    *   Use concrete language and avoid generic claims.
    *   Quantify the benefits whenever possible.
    *   Ensure the next step is easy and compelling.
4.  **Ensure Headline Alignment:**
    *   Make sure the intro supports the headline's promise and provides context.
5.  **Visual Audit:**
    *   Use the `visual_audit` tool to check the visual appeal of the first screen after making changes.

**Example:**

**Current Headline:** "Revolutionize Your Email Marketing with AI"

**Current Intro:** "In today's fast-paced world, businesses are constantly looking for ways to improve their marketing efforts. AI offers a powerful solution, but many marketers don't know where to start. Our comprehensive guide will show you how to leverage AI to boost your email marketing campaigns."

**Revised Intro:** "Struggling with low email open rates and click-through rates? AI-powered email marketing can help you personalize messages, automate campaigns, and increase conversions by up to 30%. Discover the exact AI tools and strategies we use to get results, starting with setting up your first AI-driven A/B test."
