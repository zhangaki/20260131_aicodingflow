## AI Tool Usage Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you're leveraging AI tools effectively for SEO and productivity:

*   **Keyword Research:**
    *   [ ] **PASS** | **FAIL** - Identified at least 5 relevant keywords using an AI-powered keyword research tool (e.g., Semrush, Ahrefs, or our [AI-powered keyword research tools](/tools/keyword-research)).
    *   **Criterion:** Keywords should have a search volume greater than 100/month and a keyword difficulty score less than 30.

*   **Content Optimization:**
    *   [ ] **PASS** | **FAIL** - Used an AI writing assistant (e.g., Jasper, Copy.ai) to generate or optimize content for each target keyword.
    *   **Criterion:** Content should be well-written, engaging, and optimized for the target keyword, as determined by an SEO audit (Use our [SEO Audit Tool](/tools/seo-audit) for analysis).

*   **SEO Audit:**
    *   [ ] **PASS** | **FAIL** - Performed a comprehensive SEO audit using our [SEO Audit Tool](/tools/seo-audit) to identify areas for improvement.
    *   **Criterion:** The audit should identify and address issues related to on-page optimization, technical SEO, and content quality.

*   **Performance Tracking:**
    *   [ ] **PASS** | **FAIL** - Tracked keyword rankings and organic traffic using an AI-powered SEO tool.
    *   **Criterion:** Monitor keyword rankings and organic traffic to assess the effectiveness of your SEO efforts. Use data to refine strategy.
