## Are You Choosing the Right LLM Providers Tool?

We switched our core development over to Claude 4.6 Opus for a recent client project to see if it lived up to the noise. Here's what we found.

### The Gemini Breakdown
**Best integration with Google ecosystem and longest context**

> Video Analysis: Gemini 1.5 Pro is currently the only model that can accurately OCR code from a 10-minute tutorial video.

#### Core Strengths
- 1M+ token context window
- Deep Google Workspace integration
- Multimodal (text, image, video)
- Real-time web access

#### Why You Might Skip It
- Less capable than GPT-4 on some tasks
- Can be overly cautious
- API pricing complex

#### Starting Budget
Free tier available, Pro from $19.99/month (Google One AI Premium)

## Final Recommendation

After auditing both tools, the choice comes down to your focus. **Gemini** dominates in Google Workspace users who need AI across Gmail, Docs, and Drive, whereas **Claude 4.6 Opus** provides a superior experience for Enterprise architects and developers building autonomous agent swarms..
