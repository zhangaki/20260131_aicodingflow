If you have ADHD, staying focused isn’t a motivation problem—it’s a friction problem: unclear next steps, too many choices, and constant interruptions. The right AI tools can reduce that friction by turning vague goals into concrete tasks, blocking distractions, and nudging you back on track when you drift. In this guide you’ll learn 9 practical, ADHD-friendly focus strategies and the AI apps that support them, so you can start, stay on task, and finish more consistently.
