Suno is a ai music generation tool built by Suno Inc., founded in 2022. Its core promise is simple: **Most capable AI for generating complete songs with vocals**.

The first few days with Suno involved a noticeable adjustment period. The interface is intuitive for the basics, but unlocking the full potential required reading documentation and experimenting with advanced settings. By day five, we had developed efficient patterns that significantly accelerated our output.

Mid-way through the test, we deliberately pushed Suno with edge cases and high-volume workloads. It handled most scenarios gracefully, though we did encounter occasional slowdowns during peak usage hours. The reliability over the full testing period was strong -- we experienced zero critical failures.

By the end of our month-long test, Suno had become a natural part of our workflow. The features that initially seemed like nice-to-haves turned out to be genuine productivity multipliers once we learned to use them effectively.
