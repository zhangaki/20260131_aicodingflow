## Content Creation Checklist

- **[ ] Content Quality:** Does the content provide value to the reader? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Originality:** Is the content original and not plagiarized? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Accuracy:** Is the information accurate and up-to-date? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Clarity:** Is the content clear, concise, and easy to understand? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Engagement:** Does the content engage the reader and encourage interaction? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] SEO Optimization:** Is the content optimized for search engines? (Pass/Fail)
  - See our [AEO Audit Tool](/tools/aeo-audit) for detailed SEO analysis.
- **[ ] Call to Action:** Does the content include a clear call to action? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Visual Appeal:** Is the content visually appealing and easy to read? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Mobile Friendliness:** Is the content mobile-friendly? (Pass/Fail)
- **[ ] Grammar and Spelling:** Is the content free of grammatical errors and spelling mistakes? (Pass/Fail)
