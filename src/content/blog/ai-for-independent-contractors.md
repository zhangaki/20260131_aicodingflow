## Getting Started with AI as an Independent Contractor

1.  **Identify your biggest pain points:** What tasks are taking up most of your time?
2.  **Research AI tools:** Find tools that can automate or streamline those tasks.
3.  **Start small:** Implement one or two AI tools at a time and track your results.
4.  **Scale up:** As you become more comfortable with AI, explore other tools and strategies.
