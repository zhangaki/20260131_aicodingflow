## Hands-On Analysis: Sora

**Most realistic AI video generation, directly from OpenAI**

### What We Liked
- Text-to-video generation
- Up to 1080p resolution
- 1 minute video length
- Realistic physics simulation

### The Hard Truth (Limitations)
- Limited to ChatGPT subscribers
- Watermarked output
- Long generation times

### Operational Cost
$20/month (with ChatGPT Plus)

> [!TIP]
> Creative Note: Sora's physics simulation is superior to Kling, but it currently lacks the fine-grained motion brush controls of Runway.

## Hands-On Analysis: Runway

**Professional video editing suite with AI generation**

### What We Liked
- Gen-3 Alpha video model
- Video-to-video transformation
- Green screen removal
- Professional editing tools

### The Hard Truth (Limitations)
- Credit-based system can be expensive
- Learning curve for full suite
- Some features still in beta

### Operational Cost
Free tier available, Pro from $12/month

> [!TIP]
> Pro Tip: Use the 'Motion Brush' for cinematic b-roll; it gives you much more control than simple text prompts.

## Verdict: The Better Long-Term Investment

Based on our 2026 testing:
- **Choose Sora if:** Content creators who need high-quality, realistic video clips. 
- **Choose Runway if:** Video editors who want AI tools integrated into their workflow.

Investing time in either Sora or Runway will likely pay dividends, but one definitely has a slight edge in usability.
