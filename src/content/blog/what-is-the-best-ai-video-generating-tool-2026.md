## FAQ

**Q: Can AI video generators completely replace human video editors?**

A: Not yet. While AI video generators can automate many aspects of video creation, they still lack the creative vision and nuanced understanding of human editors. For complex or highly stylized videos, human editors are still essential.

**Q: Are AI-generated videos copyright-free?**

A: Copyright law is still evolving in the context of AI-generated content. Generally, the copyright holder is the person or entity who created the AI model or who provided the creative input for the video. Always check the terms of service of the AI video generator you're using to understand the copyright implications.

**Q: What are the ethical considerations of using AI video generators?**

A: Some ethical concerns include the potential for deepfakes, the spread of misinformation, and the displacement of human video editors. It's crucial to use AI video generators responsibly and ethically, being transparent about the use of AI and avoiding the creation of misleading or harmful content.

**Q: What type of hardware do I need to run AI video generators?**

A: Most AI video generators are cloud-based, so you don't need powerful hardware to run them. A stable internet connection and a modern web browser are typically sufficient. However, if you're using AI for motion capture or animation, you may need a high-quality webcam or motion capture suit.
