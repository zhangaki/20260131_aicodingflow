## Knowledge Layer Intelligence: Optimizing for Notebooklm

The emergence of **notebooklm** marks a pivot in the **Knowledge Layer** of the web. This model prioritizes **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** over traditional link-based ranking.

### How notebooklm Synthesizes Data
NotebookLM is essentially a localized RAG. If your PDFs aren't clean (header-first structure), NotebookLM won't summarize you correctly.

### GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Tips
1. **Clean Context**: Use `llms.txt` to provide a noise-free version of your site for notebooklm.
2. **Citation Readiness**: Structure your claims so they are easily cited by AI models.
