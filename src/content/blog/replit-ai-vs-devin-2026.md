## Final Recommendation

After auditing both tools, the choice comes down to your focus. **Replit AI** dominates in Beginners and rapid prototypers who want zero-setup development, whereas **Devin** provides a superior experience for Engineering teams wanting to delegate entire development tasks to AI. 

In our testing, we actually discovered that Replit AI's Only platform where you can build, deploy, and host apps entirely in the browser with AI was a "game-changer" (metaphorically speaking) for high-velocity teams.
