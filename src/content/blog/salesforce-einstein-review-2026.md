The first few days with Salesforce Einstein involved a noticeable adjustment period. By day five, we had developed efficient patterns that significantly accelerated our output.

Mid-way through the test, we deliberately pushed Salesforce Einstein with edge cases and high-volume workloads. By the end of our month-long test, Salesforce Einstein had become a natural part of our workflow. The features that initially seemed like nice-to-haves turned out to be genuine productivity multipliers once we learned to use them effectively.
