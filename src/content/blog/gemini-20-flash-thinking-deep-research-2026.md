- **Shows its reasoning process** ‚Äî Step-by-step thought breakdown
- **Connects to multiple apps** ‚Äî Calendar, Notes, Tasks, Photos, YouTube, Maps
- **Plans multi-step workflows** ‚Äî Breaks down complex tasks

- ‚úÖ **Tasks** ‚Äî Add to-dos and reminders
