I've been testing Fireflies.ai on several side projects lately, and the real-world performance is impressive compared to the marketing hype.

## Final Recommendation

After auditing both tools, the choice comes down to your focus. **Notion AI** dominates in Teams already using Notion who want AI-enhanced productivity, whereas **Fireflies.ai** provides a superior experience for Sales teams who need searchable meeting intelligence. 

In our testing, we actually discovered that Notion AI's AI deeply integrated into the most popular all-in-one workspace was a "game-changer" (metaphorically speaking) for high-velocity teams.
