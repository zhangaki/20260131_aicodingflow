### Step 2: Define Your Creating Professional Graphics Objectives Clearly

Before asking Leonardo AI to do anything, write down exactly what you want to accomplish. For creating professional graphics, this means specifying:

With your objectives defined, it is time to engage Leonardo AI's primary capability: **Web-based interface**.

Start with a clear, specific input. Vague requests produce vague results. Instead of asking for generic output, provide context about your requirements, audience, and desired format.

Key principle: your first output from Leonardo AI is rarely your final output. Think of it as a high-quality first draft that you will refine. This mindset shift -- from expecting perfection to expecting a strong starting point -- is crucial for productive use.

### Step 4: Iterate and Refine Your Results

The iteration phase is where Leonardo AI truly shines for creating professional graphics. Take your initial output and evaluate it against your objectives from Step 2.

Common refinement strategies:

- **Reference-based refinement**: Share examples of your desired output style and ask Leonardo AI to adjust accordingly.

Most professional users go through 2-4 iteration cycles before reaching a satisfactory result. This is normal and expected -- not a sign that the tool is underperforming.

## Mistakes That Will Cost You Time

Learn from our errors:

### 1. Using Leonardo AI Without Clear Objectives

The most common mistake we see is launching Leonardo AI and improvising. Without defined goals, you end up iterating aimlessly and wasting time. Always complete the objective-setting step before generating any output.

### 2. Accepting First-Pass Output Without Review

AI-generated content from Leonardo AI can look polished on the surface while containing subtle errors, inconsistencies, or hallucinations. Always review and verify output before using it in any professional context.

### 3. Over-Prompting or Under-Prompting

There is a sweet spot for input detail. Too little context produces generic results. Too much context can confuse the model or cause it to fixate on irrelevant details. Aim for clear, specific, and concise inputs.

### 4. Ignoring Leonardo AI's Advanced Features

Many users stick to the basic functionality and miss the advanced features that deliver the most value for creating professional graphics. Invest time in learning the full feature set -- the ROI is substantial.

### 5. Not Iterating Enough

Professional results typically require 2-4 refinement cycles. Users who expect perfection on the first try often get frustrated and abandon Leonardo AI prematurely. Treat the first output as a draft, not a deliverable.
