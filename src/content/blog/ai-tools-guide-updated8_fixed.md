## Execution Utility: “Ship Your Next Post” Checklist (Pass/Fail)

Use this to choose *one* AI writing tool and publish a post you can measure. Every item is testable. If anything fails, fix it before you hit publish.

### 1) Pick the job (1 minute)

- Pass: you can finish this sentence: “For this post, I’m using AI to **(draft / outline / optimize / repurpose / research)**.”
- Fail: you’re trying to do 3+ jobs at once.

### 2) Select one tool (2 minutes)

- Pass: you pick one primary tool from the table and write down why (e.g., “Surfer SEO for on-page optimization”).
- Fail: you can’t explain why this tool is the best fit.

### 3) Generate an outline (10 minutes)

- Pass: you have **5–8 H2s** that map to reader intent (setup, steps, examples, pricing, FAQ).
- Fail: outline is a single block of text or only 1–2 headings.

### 4) Draft the core section (20–40 minutes)

- Pass: you have **at least 800 words** and **at least 1 concrete example** (prompt, screenshot description, or before/after snippet).
- Fail: it’s mostly generic claims with no examples.

### 5) Add internal links (3 minutes)

- Pass: you include **2+ internal links** that help the reader complete the workflow.
- Fail: links are missing, or anchors are vague like “click here.”

### 6) Metadata + headings check (3–5 minutes)

- Pass: title is unique and includes the main query; meta description states “who it’s for + outcome” in 1–2 sentences; page has exactly one H1.
- Fail: missing/duplicate title, empty description, or multiple H1s.

### 7) Publish + measure (5 minutes)

- Pass: you record **publish date**, **primary query**, and **CTR/ranking baseline** (even if it’s “not ranking yet”).
- Fail: you publish without a baseline, so you can’t tell what worked.
