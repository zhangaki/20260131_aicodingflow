## 1. AI-Powered Writing Assistants

### Tool:
*   Grammarly, ProWritingAid, Rytr
### Automation:
*   Grammar and spelling checks, style suggestions, tone detection, content generation.
### Actionable Checklist:

*   **Grammar Accuracy:** Upload a short story (around 500 words) containing 10 specific grammatical errors (e.g., subject-verb agreement, incorrect pronoun usage). Does the AI identify and suggest corrections for at least 9 out of 10 errors? **(Pass/Fail: Identify >=9 errors)**. (Record the errors and corrections).
*   **Tone Adjustment:** Input the sentence: "I am very disappointed with the service." Can the AI suggest at least three alternative phrasings to make the tone more professional or neutral? **(Pass/Fail: Suggest >=3 alternatives)**. (Record the suggested alternatives and evaluate their effectiveness).
*   **Style Suggestions:** Does the AI provide suggestions for improving sentence structure and clarity in a technical document? **(Pass/Fail: Provides actionable suggestions)**. (Test with a paragraph from a technical manual and note the types of suggestions provided).

## 2. AI Email Management

### Tool:
*   Superhuman, SaneBox, Mailbutler
### Automation:
*   Email prioritization, smart replies, collaboration, scheduling.
### Actionable Checklist:

*   **Prioritization Accuracy:** Can the AI tool correctly identify and prioritize 80% of high-priority emails based on sender, subject, and keywords? **(Pass/Fail: >=80% Accuracy)** (Test with a sample of 50 emails and record the number of correctly prioritized emails).
*   **Smart Reply Suggestions:** Does the AI tool provide at least three relevant and contextually appropriate smart reply suggestions for a complex email requiring a detailed response? **(Pass/Fail: Provides >=3 relevant suggestions)**. (Test with a real-world email and evaluate the quality of the suggestions).
*   **Thread Summarization:** Does the AI tool summarize email threads longer than 15 emails into a concise summary of fewer than 75 words, capturing the key decisions and action items? **(Pass/Fail: Concise summary <75 words)**. (Test with three different email threads and assess the summary quality).

## 3. AI Task Management

### Tool:
*   Todoist, Asana, ClickUp
### Automation:
*   Task assignment, progress tracking, deadline reminders, subtask creation.
### Actionable Checklist:

*   **Automated Assignment:** Can the AI tool automatically assign tasks to team members based on their skills, workload, and availability with at least 75% accuracy? **(Pass/Fail: >=75% Accuracy)** (Input 20 tasks with varying skill requirements and verify assignment accuracy, noting any misassignments).
*   **Progress Tracking & Reminders:** Does the AI tool provide real-time progress updates and send deadline reminders via both email and in-app notifications at least 24 hours and 1 hour before the deadline? **(Pass/Fail: Timely Reminders via Email & App)** (Create a task with a deadline and verify the timeliness and delivery method of reminders).
*   **Dependency Management:** Can the AI tool identify and flag dependencies between tasks, ensuring that dependent tasks are not started before their prerequisites are completed? **(Pass/Fail: Correctly Identifies Dependencies)** (Create a series of dependent tasks and verify that the AI tool correctly identifies and manages the dependencies).
