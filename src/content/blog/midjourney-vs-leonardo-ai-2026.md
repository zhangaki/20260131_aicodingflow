## Deep Dive: Midjourney
**Highest aesthetic quality in AI image generation**

- Text-to-image generation
- Style consistency
- Upscaling to 4K
- Vary and pan controls

**Operational Constraints:**
- Discord-only interface
- No API access
- Limited editing tools

## Deep Dive: Leonardo AI
**Best for game art and design assets with intuitive web interface**

- Web-based interface
- Fine-tuned models for games/design
- Canvas editor
- Motion generation

**Operational Constraints:**
- Credit system can be limiting
- Less photorealistic than Midjourney
- Smaller community

## Verdict Summary

**Choose Midjourney if:** Artists and designers who need photorealistic or stylized imagery.
**Choose Leonardo AI if:** Game developers and designers needing consistent asset generation.

Our testing suggests that while both are capable, Midjourney and Leonardo AI cater to slightly different developer personas.
