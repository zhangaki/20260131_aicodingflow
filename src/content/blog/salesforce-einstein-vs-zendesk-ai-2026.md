After auditing both tools, the choice comes down to your focus. **Salesforce Einstein** dominates in Enterprise sales teams already on Salesforce who want AI-driven insights, whereas **Zendesk AI** provides a superior experience for Customer support teams wanting to reduce ticket volume with AI.
