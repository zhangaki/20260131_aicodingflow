## 1. The Physics: The Solar Constant

The Sun emits approximately $3.8 \times 10^{26}$ Watts of power.
Earth intercepts about one two-billionth of that.
We are currently wasting $99.9999999\%$ of the available energy in our neighborhood. For an AI-driven civilization, this is an engineering failure.

**The Solution: The Dyson Swarm**:
Forget the solid shell (a "Dyson Sphere" is physically impossible due to tensile strength limits).
We will build a **Swarm** of millions of autonomous satellite collectors in a tight orbit around the Sun. They beam energy via microwave or laser to central processing hubs‚Äî**Matrioshka Brains**.

## 2. The Tech Stack: The Matrioshka Brain

A Matrioshka Brain is a massive computer that surrounds a star and consumes its entire energy output.
It consists of multiple shells:
-   **Inner Shell**: Captures high-energy radiation directly from the Sun to power the fastest compute nodes.
-   **Outer Shell**: Captures the waste heat (waste energy) from the inner shell to power slower, more efficient processes.
Each layer "recycles" the energy. It is the ultimate expression of **Computational Efficiency**.

**The Substrate**:
We won't use silicon. We will use **Optical Computing** or **Neuromorphic Glass**.
The goal is to turn the "dead" matter of Mercury and the Asteroid Belt into "living" processors. Mercury, being a rocky planet close to the Sun, is our primary source of raw materials for the swarm.

def calculate_omega_capacity(coverage_percentage):
    """
    Calculates the compute capacity based on a Dyson Swarm's coverage.
    """
