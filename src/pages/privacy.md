As a static site generated by Astro:

## 3. Cookies & Analytics
We use **Google Analytics 4** to track aggregate usage (page views, session duration, geographic distribution). This helps us understand what content is most useful. Google Analytics may use cookies to distinguish unique users. We do not use pixel tracking for ad retargeting.

We may also display advertisements through **Google AdSense**, which uses cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).
