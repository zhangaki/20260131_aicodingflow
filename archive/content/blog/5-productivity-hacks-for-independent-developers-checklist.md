# 5 Productivity Hacks for Independent Developers

Independent developers often struggle with distractions and endless tasks, leading to lost time and reduced output. This guide provides 5 actionable productivity hacks to help you reclaim your time, focus on your priorities, and achieve more. Start by implementing time blocking to structure your day and eliminate context switching.

## 1. Time Blocking

Time blocking involves scheduling specific blocks of time for specific tasks. This helps you stay focused and avoid context switching.
