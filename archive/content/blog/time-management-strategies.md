# Time Management Strategies for Independent Developers

Here are some time management strategies to help you be more productive:

## Checklist:

- [ ] **Prioritize tasks:** Do you have a clear list of your tasks, ranked by priority?
   - **Pass:** Yes, I have a prioritized task list.
   - **Fail:** No, I don't have a prioritized task list.

- [ ] **Time blocking:** Do you schedule specific blocks of time for specific tasks?
   - **Pass:** Yes, I use time blocking.
   - **Fail:** No, I don't use time blocking.

- [ ] **Eliminate distractions:** Do you minimize distractions while working?
   - **Pass:** Yes, I minimize distractions.
   - **Fail:** No, I don't minimize distractions.

- [ ] **Take breaks:** Do you take regular breaks to avoid burnout?
   - **Pass:** Yes, I take regular breaks.
   - **Fail:** No, I don't take regular breaks.

- [ ] **Use time tracking tools:** Do you use tools to track how you spend your time?
   - **Pass:** Yes, I use time tracking tools.
   - **Fail:** No, I don't use time tracking tools.
