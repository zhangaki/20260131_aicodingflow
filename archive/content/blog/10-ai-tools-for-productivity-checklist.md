# Productivity AI Tool Implementation Checklist

This checklist will help you evaluate whether you are effectively using AI tools to boost your productivity. For each tool, review the criteria and determine if you are meeting the standards for effective implementation.

## 1. Taskify AI

*   **Criteria:** Are you actively using Taskify AI to manage your tasks and to-do lists?
*   **Pass:** You consistently use Taskify AI to prioritize tasks, set deadlines, and track your progress. You regularly review and update your task lists.
*   **Fail:** You are not actively using Taskify AI, or you only use it sporadically. Your task lists are outdated or incomplete.
*   **[Learn more about Taskify AI here](/taskify-ai-review)**
